Accept payments from any channel through a single Flowdexx integration — with real-time reporting, automatic reconciliation, and T+1 settlement.

Send money to hundreds of recipients simultaneously — salaries, vendor payments, refunds, and marketplace payouts — from a single Flowdexx API call.

FAQ
Straight answers.

Questions we get asked most often. If yours isn't here, just reach out — we respond within 24 hours.

How long does a typical web or app project take?

A Starter Website takes 2–4 weeks. A Business Platform 6–10 weeks. Enterprise and SaaS projects run 12–24 weeks depending on complexity. Mobile apps follow similar timelines. We provide a detailed project schedule during the scoping call before any commitment is made.

Can I use Flowdexx without building with Deadlock AI?

Yes. Flowdexx is available as a standalone product with its own REST API and documentation. Any developer can integrate it into any existing platform. Clients who also use our development services receive Flowdexx at preferential commercial rates.

Are the prices on your pricing page fixed?

The prices listed are starting rates for the described scope. Every project is individually scoped and quoted after a short discovery call. We issue a fixed-price proposal — no hourly billing, no surprise invoices at the end of a sprint.

What is included in post-launch support?

Post-launch support covers bug fixes, minor content updates, performance monitoring, and technical assistance. The duration and scope depend on your package. For ongoing feature development, we offer monthly retainer arrangements that suit most growing businesses.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?

Yes. We work with clients in the UAE, UK, Saudi Arabia, and beyond — particularly for businesses with Pakistan-facing operations or those leveraging our team's expertise and cost advantage for their digital build. All communication is remote-friendly.

Is Flowdexx PCI DSS certified?

Yes. Flowdexx is PCI DSS certified and implements AML/KYC controls on all transactions. The platform uses AES-256 encryption and TLS 1.3 in transit. For specific compliance or regulatory questions, please contact our team directly.
